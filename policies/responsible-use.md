# Responsible Use Policy

Open Intelligence Kit is for low-frequency public-source research and evidence preservation. It is not a bypass or mass-scraping toolkit.

## Allowed by default

- Public webpages.
- Official docs, release notes, changelogs, standards, laws, platform rules.
- GitHub repositories, package registries, and public code releases.
- Company/product pages and public reports.
- User-provided public links.
- Low-frequency manual research runs.

## Requires explicit permission and additional review

- Long-running monitors or scheduled crawlers.
- Large URL lists.
- Dynamic pages that require browser interaction.
- Price pages, marketplace listings, or competitor pages at scale.
- Public social pages used only as qualitative samples.

## Forbidden by default

- Login automation without explicit authorization.
- Bypassing captchas, paywalls, access controls, or anti-bot systems.
- Proxy pools, fingerprint spoofing, or evasion tooling.
- Bulk comment scraping or social-media harvesting.
- Collecting private, personal, sensitive, or regulated data.
- High-frequency crawling that may burden a site.
- Ignoring a site's robots.txt or terms of service.

## Reporting rule

If a source is inaccessible, blocked, protected, or unreliable, record that limitation instead of bypassing it.
